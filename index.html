Our Programs

We meet kids at their level
regardless of their age

Creche

(18 months-1 plus years)

Our Creche program is designed for children aged 18 months to 1 plus years, providing a safe and nurturing environment for their early development.

Pre-School

(1 plus-3 years)

In our Pre-School program, tailored for children aged 1 plus to 3 years, we focus on fostering early social, cognitive, and motor skills through play-based learning activities.

Nursery

(3-5 years)

The Nursery program, designed for children aged 3 to 5 years, emphasizes a blend of structured learning and creative exploration to prepare students for primary education.

Grade

(5-10 years)

Our Grade program, catering to children aged 5 to 10 years, offers a comprehensive curriculum that promotes academic excellence, critical thinking, and personal growth.
